To enable autofix for all projects in your Semgrep organization:

1. In the Cloud Platform, go to **[Settings](https://semgrep.dev/orgs/-/settings)**.
2. Under **Code**, toggle **Autofix** <i class="fa-solid fa-toggle-large-on"></i> on. You can also choose to include Assistant-written fixes; if so, check the appropriate box and select the confidence level to adjust the quality vs. quantity of provided suggestions.
